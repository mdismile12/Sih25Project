## 📊 THE DOSAGE FORMULA

```
CALCULATED DOSAGE = DOSAGE RATE (mg/kg) × ANIMAL WEIGHT (kg)

Examples:
  Amoxycillin: 15 × 50 = 750 mg
  Enrofloxacin: 10 × 50 = 500 mg
  Oxytetracycline: 20 × 50 = 1000 mg
```
